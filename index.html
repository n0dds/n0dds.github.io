Hi, I'm N0dds. An artist/illustrator currently based in Leicestershire, England.

I have a background in freelance illustration, audio & video production and game design. I also have an intense passion for Folklore and History, having read many collected texts on the subject and using it as a basis for my much of my art and other projects.

The bulk of my art is done digitally, but in the past few years I've made something of a return to tradtional mediums. My favourite is watercolour, with the occasional dabble in gouache, ink, marker and pencil.
